#  Amazon Prime Video Dashboard (Power BI)

##  Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Amazon Prime Video content. The dashboard provides insights into movies and TV shows based on genres, ratings, release years, and geographical distribution.

---

##  Objectives

* Analyze total shows available on Prime Video
* Understand distribution of Movies vs TV Shows
* Explore genres and ratings popularity
* Identify trends over years
* Visualize global content distribution

---

##  Dataset Information

The dataset includes:

* Title
* Genre
* Rating
* Release Year
* Country
* Type (Movie / TV Show)

---

##  Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key Metrics

* Total Titles: 9655
* Total Ratings: 25
* Total Genres: 519
* Start Year: 1920
* End Year: 2021

### 🔹 Visualizations

* Ratings Distribution
* Genre-wise Content Analysis
* Total Shows by Country (Map View 🌍)
* Movies vs TV Shows (Donut Chart)
* Content Growth Over Years

* Drama and Comedy are top genres
* Significant growth in content after 2000
